Information We Collect and Children's Privacy

Math Rush is designed to be fully safe and enjoyable for users of all ages, including children. We strictly comply with Google Play’s Families Policy and the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

Usage Data

Usage Data is collected automatically when using the Application. This includes solely non-personally identifiable technical information:

  • Device configuration (model, OS version)
  • Crash logs and technical diagnostics
  • Gameplay statistics (e.g., puzzle scores, levels completed)
  • Application performance data

We do not collect any personally identifiable information (such as names, email addresses, phone numbers, or precise location). Furthermore, for younger audiences and child-directed traffic, we do not transmit or collect persistent device identifiers or advertising identifiers (AAID) for behavioral tracking.

How We Use Information

Collected non-personal information is used strictly to:

  • Provide, maintain, and support the Application
  • Improve the educational gameplay experience
  • Analyze application stability and fix technical issues
  • Serve contextually appropriate, family-friendly advertisements
  • Manage optional premium purchases via secure methods

Third-Party Services and Advertising

The Application utilizes trusted third-party SDKs to support its functionality. These services are strictly configured to treat data in accordance with children's privacy regulations:

  • Google AdMob: Advertisements may be displayed. For children and younger audiences, the application automatically requests non-personalized, contextual-only advertisements that are family-friendly. Behavioral or targeted tracking is completely disabled.
  • Firebase Analytics: Used solely to collect aggregated, anonymous gameplay statistics and crash diagnostics to improve app performance. No personal tracking is performed.
  • Google Play Billing: Optional premium features or ad-removal purchases are available. All financial transactions are securely processed entirely by Google Play. We do not collect, view, or store any payment or financial information.
